Kevin Durant: The Slim Reaper


Kevin Durant (born September 29, 1988, Washington, D.C., U.S.) is an American professional basketball player who won the 2013–14 National Basketball Association (NBA) Most Valuable Player (MVP) award and established himself as one of the best players of his generation while only in his early 20s.
