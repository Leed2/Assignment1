LeBron James: The King


LeBron James was born on December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio. Widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time, LeBron began his NBA career in 2003 with the Cleveland Cavaliers. Over the course of his illustrious career, he has played for the Miami Heat, returned to the Cavaliers, and is currently playing for the Los Angeles Lakers. Known for his versatility, athleticism, and basketball IQ, LeBron has become a global icon, both on and off the court.
